What is actually in "Natural Flavor"?

1/28/2018

0 Comments

 

Not-So-Natural Flavors

If you want a straight answer, here it is: "NATURAL FLAVOR" IS ALMOST NEVER ACTUALLY NATURAL!

Sugar, even in the 1st and 2nd ingredient, is almost always WAY healthier than "Natural Flavor"! Unless the product is USDA Certified Organic and Non-GMO, steer away from "Natural Flavor" at all costs. Most "Natural Flavor" is not natural at all, in fact, it's usually an artificial chemical something-or-other.

Did you know? Natural Raspberry Flavor is actually beaver s**t, AKA: Castoreum? Well, now you do.

While most companies hide what is in the ever mysterious term "Natural Flavor", some (such as IZZE and Blue Sky) will reveal what mix of ingredients is contained in the so-called "Natural Flavor", either on the product packaging (usually contained in a pair of parentheses just after the ingredient "Natural Flavor", such as on the label shown below) or on their website (example below).
